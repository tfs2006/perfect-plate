Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Introduction

Welcome to Perfect Plate. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data and tell you about your privacy rights.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Email address and name (for account creation)
  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)
  • Usage data (meal generation history, preferences)
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type, device information)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve our meal planning services
  • Process your payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send you service updates and notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Payment information is processed through Stripe and we never store your full credit card details.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Export your data

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and authentication. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database hosting
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Google Gemini AI: Meal plan generation
  • Vercel: Application hosting

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at: perfectplate@4ourmedia.com
